# HRdashboard
This project presents an interactive Human Resources Dashboard built using Tableau to analyze employee demographics, attrition trends, and job satisfaction levels.
The dashboard helps HR teams and management identify workforce patterns, attrition risks, and employee satisfaction insights to support data-driven HR decisions.

Dashboard Features:

KPI Summary Cards – Displays total employees, active employees, attrition count, attrition rate, and average age at a glance.

Education Filter – Allows dynamic filtering of the entire dashboard based on employee education background.

Attrition by Gender – Visual comparison of attrition counts between male and female employees.

Department-wise Attrition – Shows the proportion of employee attrition across HR, R&D, and Sales departments.

Employees by Age Group – Analyzes workforce distribution across different age bands using binned histograms.

Job Satisfaction by Role – Heatmap representation of employee satisfaction ratings across job roles.

Education-wise Attrition – Compares attrition levels across different educational qualifications.

Attrition by Gender & Age Group – Donut charts illustrating gender-based attrition trends across age categories.

Interactive Insights – Enables cross-filtering, tooltips, and drill-down analysis for deeper exploration.
